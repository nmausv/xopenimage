bitmap formats
depth of 1

			left			background		foreground

X11 (.xbm)		least-sig-bit		0 = white		1 = black


NetPBM (.pbm)		
 ASCII P1		literal left-to-right	'0' = white		'1' = black
 binary P4		most-sig-bit		0 = white		1 = black

 binary PBM requires bit-order reversal


TIFF			most-sig-bit
 miniswhite					0 = white		1 = black
 minisblack					1 = white		0 = black

 TIFF requires bit-order reversal,
  AND if minisblack, then bitwise complement (~)

PNG			most-sig-bit		1 = white		0 = black

 PNG requires bit-order reversal AND bitwise complement (~)
